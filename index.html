INFERNO CORE

Seb Prime 100 - 147th Moon of Saturn

The Biome

A vast volcanic landscape of osidian rock and molten lava rivers. Temperature reaches 2,400C at the crater's core. The geothermal energy powering the entire planet originates here.

Guardian - Ignis

The Phoenix Xeron lives for 333 years before combusting in the volcanic crater. From its ashes, new life erupts within 72hours. Its feathers conduct geothermal energy across the planet.

Flora

Xenite Bloom - crystal flowers that absorbs CO2. Ashroot Tree - indestructible black trees grown from phoenix ash. Embervine - glowing vines feeding on lava heat.

Experiences

Magma Gondola - Crystal Mine T our - Phoenix Watch Platform - Heat Lab - Lava River Cruise

AZURE ABYSS

Seb Prime 100 - 147th Moon of Saturn

The Biome

A vast bioluminescent ocean warmed by underground lava tunnels from Inferno Core. Crystal coral reefs grow on obsidian glass formed where lava meets water at The Rift.

Guardian - Krakenis

An ancient being built from coral and cosmic obsidian. Its tentacles reach the thermal tunnels connecting both biomes. Without Krakenis, the ocean would freeze.

Flora

Luminos Coral - reef that glows and plays music. Tideweed - algae changing colour with emotions. Crystal Kelp - underwater crystal forest instead of seaweed.

Experiences

Deep Dive Pod - Crystal Reef Walk - Bioluminescene Night Swim - Mood Fish Aquarium - Surface Safari
